What this is

A personal tool to track ASX and US market hours, plus upcoming earnings for major companies. All times converted to Sydney local time.

Upcoming Earnings

The home page shows earnings from major companies (Mag 7, S&P 100) plus your watchlist. Window auto-expands from 7 to 180 days until at least 5 items are found.

Search by ticker (AAPL) or company name (apple, microsoft) — case-insensitive.

Market Hours

  • ASX Pre-open 7:00 – 10:00 / Open 10:00 – 16:00 AEST
  • US Pre-market NY 04:00 – 09:30
  • US Open NY 09:30 – 16:00
  • US Post-market NY 16:00 – 20:00

Earnings Time Conversion

BMO (Pre-market) earnings typically released NY 07:00 → Sydney evening same day. AMC (After close) earnings typically NY 17:00 (earnings call) → Sydney 7-8 AM next day.

Data Sources

Earnings: Finnhub.io (180 days forward), updated daily via GitHub Actions. Major company calendars manually verified to ensure Mag 7 always appear.

All calculations run locally in your browser. Watchlist saved in localStorage — does not sync across devices.

Disclaimer

For reference only, not investment advice. Pre-market and after-hours trading have low liquidity and high volatility. Verify important decisions against official sources.
